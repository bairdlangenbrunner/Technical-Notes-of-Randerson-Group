Greenplanet¶

Randerson group has nodes from -10-251 to -10-254, and reservation for 48 cores on -2-9. Name of queue is randerson_f.

Data¶

Due to the space limit on hard drives, datasets are stored in different places. Generally it might not be a good idea to store your data in your home directory, as there are smaller space allocated for the drive home directory. You can make symbolic link in your data directory to keep organized; see Symbolic Link.
